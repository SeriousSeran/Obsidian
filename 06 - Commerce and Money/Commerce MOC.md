## The sequence

Do not start with advanced investing.

Start with:

1. Cash flow
2. Debt control
3. Expense awareness
4. Emergency buffer
5. Active income
6. Semi-passive products
7. Investing
8. Business ownership
9. Equity and assets

## The most important distinction

Income is not wealth.

Wealth comes from:

- Keeping surplus
- Owning assets
- Building systems
- Creating products
- Controlling expenses
- Avoiding destructive debt

## Medical analogy

A person can have a normal-looking income and still be financially shocked.

Cash flow is circulation.

Debt is bleeding.

Emergency fund is reserve capacity.

Investing is rehabilitation, not resuscitation.
