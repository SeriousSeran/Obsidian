# Cardiovascular Examination Station

> OSCE station map for cardiovascular examination. Not final clinical advice.

## Opening
- Identify patient, wash hands, explain, consent.

## Focused task
- Inspect: general appearance, hands, face, neck.
- Palpate: pulses, apex beat, heaves, thrills.
- Auscultate: 4 valve areas, axilla, carotids.

## Findings
- State present and absent clinical findings plainly.

## Differentials
- Consider murmurs, heart failure signs.

## Plan
- Investigations: ECG, Echocardiogram.
- Review and escalation as necessary.
