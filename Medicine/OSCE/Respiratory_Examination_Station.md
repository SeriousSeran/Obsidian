# Respiratory Examination Station

> OSCE station map for respiratory examination. Not final clinical advice.

## Opening
- Identify patient, wash hands, explain, consent.

## Focused task
- Inspect: sputum pot, inhalers, oxygen, general appearance, hands, face, neck.
- Palpate: trachea, apex beat, chest expansion.
- Percuss: lung fields (apices to bases).
- Auscultate: breath sounds, added sounds, vocal resonance.

## Findings
- State present and absent clinical findings plainly.

## Differentials
- Consider asthma, COPD, pneumonia, effusion, fibrosis.

## Plan
- Investigations: Peak flow, ABG, Sputum culture, Chest X-ray.
- Review and escalation as necessary.
