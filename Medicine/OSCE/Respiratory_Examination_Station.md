# Respiratory Examination Station

> OSCE station map for respiratory examination. Not final clinical advice.

## Opening
- Identify patient, wash hands, explain, consent.

## Focused task
- Inspect: general appearance, sputum pots, hands (clubbing, tremor), face, neck.
- Palpate: trachea, chest expansion, vocal fremitus.
- Percuss: lung fields (anterior, lateral, posterior).
- Auscultate: breath sounds, added sounds (wheeze, crackles), vocal resonance.

## Findings
- State present and absent clinical findings plainly.

## Differentials
- Consider asthma, COPD, pneumonia, pleural effusion, pulmonary fibrosis.

## Plan
- Investigations: Sputum culture, Chest X-ray, Peak flow, Spirometry.
- Review and escalation as necessary.
