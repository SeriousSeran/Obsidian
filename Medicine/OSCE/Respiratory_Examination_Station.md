# Respiratory Examination Station

> OSCE station map for respiratory examination. Not final clinical advice.

## Opening
- Identify patient, wash hands, explain, consent.

## Focused task
- Inspect: sputum pot, inhalers, general appearance, hands (clubbing), face, chest (shape, symmetry).
- Palpate: tracheal deviation, chest expansion.
- Percuss: lung fields (anterior, lateral, posterior).
- Auscultate: lung fields (anterior, lateral, posterior), vocal resonance.

## Findings
- State present and absent clinical findings plainly.

## Differentials
- Consider asthma, COPD, pneumonia, pleural effusion, pneumothorax.

## Plan
- Investigations: Peak flow, ABG, Chest X-ray.
- Review and escalation as necessary.
