# Emergency Medicine Viva Flashcard Product

## Product concept

A focused flashcard deck for medical students and junior doctors preparing for emergency medicine viva/OSCE-style questions.

## Promise

Learn how to recognize emergency patterns, explain mechanisms, answer viva questions, and state safe immediate management.

## Suggested title

Emergency Medicine Viva: High-Yield Clinical Flashcards for Medical Students and Junior Doctors

## Target user

Final-year medical students, interns, junior doctors, OSCE/viva candidates, and students who struggle to convert textbook facts into spoken clinical answers.

## Card format

Front:

```text
A patient presents with acute breathlessness and hypotension. What are the immediate priorities?
```

Back:

```text
Recognition:
This may be shock, acute heart failure, PE, sepsis, anaphylaxis, arrhythmia, or severe asthma.

First response:
Call for help, ABCDE, oxygen if hypoxic, monitor ECG/BP/SpO2, IV access, bedside glucose, focused history/examination, treat reversible life threats.

Viva wording:
In an unstable patient, I will first stabilize using ABCDE while simultaneously looking for immediately reversible causes.
```

## Initial topics

Reduced consciousness, shock, ACS, acute heart failure, asthma, COPD, anaphylaxis, sepsis, DKA/HHS, hyperkalemia, hypoglycemia, stroke, seizure, poisoning, acute abdomen, upper GI bleeding, dengue warning signs, alcohol withdrawal and Wernicke encephalopathy.
