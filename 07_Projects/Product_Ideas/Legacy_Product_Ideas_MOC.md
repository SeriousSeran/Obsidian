# Product Ideas MOC

The first product path should use existing strengths: medicine, teaching, clinical reasoning, AI-assisted summarization, flashcards, OSCE/viva preparation, visual recognition, and systems thinking.

## Product ladder

### Level 1 - Simple digital products

PDF notes, flashcard decks, viva answer packs, OSCE image recognition packs, audio-friendly scripts, prompt packs.

### Level 2 - Community and teaching

Small paid classes, revision groups, case discussion sessions, exam coaching, feedback-based study circles.

### Level 3 - AI-assisted tools

Patient summary generator, OSCE simulator, viva examiner chatbot, flashcard generator, ward checklist assistant.

### Level 4 - Healthcare systems and devices

Device troubleshooting guides, equipment workflow systems, inventory systems, procurement support, medical technology training products.

## Product rule

Start with a small paid proof before building a big system.
