# AI Prompt Pack for Medical Students

## Product concept

A collection of prompts that help medical students use AI safely and effectively for learning, OSCE practice, viva answers, and clinical reasoning.

## Target user

Medical students, interns, junior doctors, and learners who do not know how to ask AI good medical questions.

## Prompt categories

1. Clinical reasoning
2. Case presentation
3. Viva examiner mode
4. OSCE station practice
5. Flashcard generation
6. Guideline-style summary
7. Mechanism-first explanation
8. Differential diagnosis building
9. Investigation interpretation
10. Management planning

## Example prompt

```text
Teach me this topic like a ward mentor. Start from the patient picture, then explain physiology, pathology, clinical signs, investigations, management, and viva answer. Avoid dry textbook listing unless necessary.
```

## Safety principle

AI prompts should ask for uncertainty, red flags, clinical context, and guideline checking when needed.
