# Medical Education Product Engine

## Core idea

Medical education is the best first side-income path because it uses existing strengths:

- Clinical study
- Teaching
- AI-assisted organization
- Exam awareness
- Flashcard creation
- Explanation skill
- English/Sinhala/Singlish communication
- Visual and systems thinking

## The first product principle

Do not start with a huge course.

Start with one small product that solves one painful problem.

Examples:

- Emergency viva flashcards
- ECG rapid recognition cards
- ABG interpretation cards
- OSCE image recognition pack
- Case presentation templates
- Dengue clerking and monitoring framework
- Emergency drug dose cards

## Product creation cycle

1. Pick a narrow exam pain.
2. Create a small useful version.
3. Give a sample to trusted students.
4. Ask what was useful and confusing.
5. Price modestly.
6. Improve based on real feedback.
7. Convert feedback into version 2.
8. Create related content to distribute it.

## Good product structure

Each card or note should include:

- What is seen
- What it means
- Mechanism
- Immediate danger
- Investigations
- Management
- Viva wording
- Common traps
- Flashcard question

## Distribution

Possible channels:

- Facebook posts
- TikTok short teaching clips
- WhatsApp student groups
- Telegram channel
- YouTube short lessons
- Small live teaching sessions

## Avoid

- Waiting until perfect
- Making free content forever
- Designing logos before product
- Buying tools before selling
- Making broad notes nobody urgently needs
