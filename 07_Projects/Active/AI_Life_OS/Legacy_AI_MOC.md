# AI MOC

AI is a force multiplier for medicine, learning, and product creation.

The goal is not to become dependent on AI. The goal is to use AI to build systems faster.

## Skill path

1. Prompting for structured clinical learning
2. Markdown and Obsidian workflows
3. Flashcard generation
4. Spreadsheet and database thinking
5. No-code prototypes
6. Basic Python
7. APIs
8. Product design
9. Privacy and safety
10. Healthcare workflow integration

## Useful AI roles

Clinical tutor, viva examiner, flashcard maker, summary generator, differential diagnosis trainer, OSCE station builder, product ideation partner, content repurposing assistant, research organizer.

## Warning

AI should not replace clinical responsibility. It can help organize, teach, and draft, but clinical judgement must stay with the clinician.
