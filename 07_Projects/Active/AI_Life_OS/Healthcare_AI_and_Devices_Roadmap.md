# Healthcare AI and Devices Roadmap

## Core idea

The future opportunity is at the intersection of clinical understanding, systems thinking, AI workflows, healthcare device awareness, education, and training.

## Short-term direction

Start with education products because they require low capital and use existing strengths.

Examples: flashcard products, OSCE image interpretation packs, viva simulators, case presentation templates, AI prompt packs.

## Medium-term direction

Build tools for students and doctors.

Examples: patient summary generator, ward round checklist, emergency medicine assistant, dengue monitoring tracker, discharge summary helper, prescription safety checklist, OSCE examiner simulator.

## Long-term direction

Move into healthcare systems and devices.

Examples: device troubleshooting guides, equipment workflow documentation, medical device training products, inventory and maintenance systems, procurement decision support, AI-assisted clinical documentation tools.

## Role identity

The valuable role is: doctor + clinical workflow thinker + product designer + AI system builder.

## Skills to learn

Clinical: emergency medicine, common ward presentations, risk stratification, investigation interpretation, safe management pathways.

Technical: Markdown, Obsidian, GitHub, databases, no-code tools, Python basics, APIs, web app basics.

Business: product validation, pricing, distribution, support, trust-building, sales without manipulation.
