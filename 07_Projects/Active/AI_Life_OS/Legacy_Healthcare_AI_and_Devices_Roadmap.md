# Healthcare AI and Devices Roadmap

## Core idea

The future opportunity is at the intersection of:

- Clinical understanding
- Systems thinking
- AI workflows
- Healthcare device awareness
- Education and training

## Short-term direction

Start with education products because they require low capital and use existing strengths.

Examples:

- Flashcard products
- OSCE image interpretation packs
- Viva simulators
- Case presentation templates
- AI prompt packs

## Medium-term direction

Build tools for students and doctors.

Examples:

- Patient summary generator
- Ward round checklist
- Emergency medicine assistant
- Dengue monitoring tracker
- Discharge summary helper
- Prescription safety checklist
- OSCE examiner simulator

## Long-term direction

Move into healthcare systems and devices.

Examples:

- Device troubleshooting guides
- Equipment workflow documentation
- Medical device training products
- Inventory and maintenance systems
- Procurement decision support
- AI-assisted clinical documentation tools

## Role identity

The valuable role is not just coder.

The valuable role is:

> Doctor + clinical workflow thinker + product designer + AI system builder.
