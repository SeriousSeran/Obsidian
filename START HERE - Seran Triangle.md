# START HERE - Seran Triangle

This vault is a public-safe operating map built around three connected domains:

1. **Medicine** - clinical skill, exam success, patient reasoning, credibility.
2. **Commerce and Money** - cash flow, debt control, business models, sales, investing.
3. **AI and Technology** - tools, automation, medical education products, healthcare systems.

The aim is not to collect random notes. The aim is to build a second brain that helps a future doctor become clinically excellent, financially stable, technologically capable, and eventually able to build medical education and healthcare tools.

## Core identity

> I am becoming a doctor who builds AI-assisted medical education and healthcare tools, starting with exam-focused study products and later moving into clinical workflow systems, healthcare devices, and scalable health-tech products.

## The correct order

1. Stabilize money and health.
2. Pass exams and become clinically solid.
3. Build one small useful medical education product.
4. Build a public teaching and content system.
5. Learn commerce, accounting, banking, business models, sales, and investing.
6. Prototype AI-healthcare tools.
7. Later build scalable products, systems, or businesses.

## First mission

The first mission is not passive income. The first mission is stability plus one paid proof of value.

A good first proof is:

> Create a focused Emergency Medicine Viva flashcard product and get at least one real buyer or serious user feedback.

## The operating principle

Do not try to learn everything before acting.

Build in this cycle:

**Learn -> make a small useful thing -> test with real people -> improve -> repeat.**
