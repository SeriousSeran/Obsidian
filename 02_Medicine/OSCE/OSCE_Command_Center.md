# OSCE Command Center

> A practical station map for bedside confidence, safe structure, and viva-ready explanations.

## Station Flow

| Step | Aim | Prompt |
|---|---|---|
| Opening | Safety and consent | Identify patient, wash hands, explain, consent |
| Focused task | Do the requested exam/history | Keep the sequence calm and visible |
| Findings | Say what is present and absent | Use plain clinical language |
| Differentials | Prioritize likely and dangerous causes | Explain why each is plausible |
| Plan | Investigations and management | Mention red flags, escalation, and review |

## Core Stations

- Cardiovascular examination
- Respiratory examination
- Abdominal examination
- Neurological screening
- Cranial nerve examination
- Diabetes and endocrine counselling
- Chest pain history
- Shortness of breath history
- Abdominal pain history
- Fever history
