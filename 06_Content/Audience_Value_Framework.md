# Audience Value Framework

> A structured approach to creating useful, engaging content.

## Framework Components

### 1. Who (Audience)
- **Who is this for?** Define the specific group of people you are targeting.
- **What is their current state?** What are they struggling with or looking to achieve?

### 2. Hook (Attention)
- **Why should they care?** What is the immediate promise or intriguing question?
- **How does it stand out?** What makes this unique compared to existing content?

### 3. Value (Core Message)
- **What is the key takeaway?** The one thing you want them to remember.
- **What are the supporting points?** Logical steps, evidence, or stories that reinforce the core message.
- **Is it actionable?** Can they apply it immediately?

### 4. Action (Next Step)
- **What do they do next?** A clear call to action (e.g., try a new habit, read another note, subscribe).
- **How will they know it worked?** The expected outcome of taking the action.
