# Content Brand System

## Core idea

Content should not be random entertainment. It should lead to trust, teaching, and products.

## Best niche

Medical exams, ward thinking, emergency medicine, and AI-assisted clinical learning for medical students and junior doctors.

## Content pillars

1. Clinical reasoning
2. Viva and OSCE traps
3. Emergency medicine
4. AI for medical learning
5. Case presentation
6. Mechanism-first explanations
7. Study systems

## Content format

1. Hook: name the pain or mistake.
2. Clinical explanation.
3. Why it matters in exam or ward.
4. One useful takeaway.
5. Link to product or deeper note if relevant.

## Example hook ideas

- Why listing causes is not enough in viva.
- How to answer reduced consciousness safely.
- Why cash flow is like circulation.
- Why medical students should learn AI carefully.
- How to recognize an emergency image in OSCE.

## Warning

Do not make free content forever without a product path.
