# Content Repurposing Framework

> Turn one good idea into many formats.

**Note:** Do not publish private or sensitive content. Always perform a privacy review before repurposing.

## Repurposing Pipeline

1. **Source Material**: A solid, validated idea or long-form piece.
2. **Deconstruction**: Break down the core concepts into standalone points.
3. **Reformatting**:
   - Short-form written (tweets, threads)
   - Medium-form written (newsletter, blog post)
   - Visual (infographic, diagram)
   - Audio/Video (short clip, full video script)
4. **Distribution**: Plan the channels for distribution.

## Example Flow

| Source | Short Written | Visual | Video |
|---|---|---|---|
| Deep dive article on topic | Thread of top 5 tips | Diagram of the core concept | 60-second summary short |

## Rules

- Repurpose only the best performing or most valuable ideas.
- Adjust the format to fit the platform, don't just copy-paste.
