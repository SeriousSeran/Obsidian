# Medicine MOC

Medicine is the base of Seran Triangle.

The aim is to become clinically solid, not merely exam-passing.

## Medical note style

Every major topic should be written as:

1. Patient picture
2. Normal physiology
3. Pathology
4. Clinical signs
5. Investigations
6. Management
7. Complications
8. Viva answer
9. Traps
10. Flashcards

## Principle

Never learn a sign as a dead fact.

Ask:

- What anatomy creates this sign?
- What physiology failed?
- What pathology caused it?
- What danger does it indicate?
- What should I do next?
