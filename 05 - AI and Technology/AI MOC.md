# AI MOC

AI is a force multiplier for medicine, learning, and product creation.

The goal is not to become dependent on AI. The goal is to use AI to build systems faster.

## Skill path

1. Prompting for structured clinical learning
2. Markdown and Obsidian workflows
3. Flashcard generation
4. Spreadsheet and database thinking
5. No-code prototypes
6. Basic Python
7. APIs
8. Product design
9. Privacy and safety
10. Healthcare workflow integration

## Useful AI roles

- Clinical tutor
- Viva examiner
- Flashcard maker
- Summary generator
- Differential diagnosis trainer
- OSCE station builder
- Product ideation partner
- Content repurposing assistant
- Research organizer

## Warning

AI should not replace clinical responsibility.

AI can help organize, teach, and draft, but clinical judgement must stay with the clinician.
