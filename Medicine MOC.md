# Medicine MOC

Medicine is the base of the triangle. The aim is to become clinically solid, not merely exam-passing.

## Medical note style

Every major topic should be written as:

1. Patient picture
2. Normal physiology
3. Pathology
4. Clinical signs
5. Investigations
6. Management
7. Complications
8. Viva answer
9. Traps
10. Flashcards

## Major areas

- Emergency medicine
- Cardiology
- Endocrinology
- Nephrology
- Infectious disease
- Respiratory medicine
- Neurology
- Gastroenterology
- Pharmacology
- Clinical examination
- Investigation interpretation

## Principle

Never learn a sign as a dead fact. Ask: what anatomy creates this sign, what physiology failed, what pathology caused it, what danger does it indicate, and what should I do next?
