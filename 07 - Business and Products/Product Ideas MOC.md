## Product ladder

### Level 1 - Simple digital products

- PDF notes
- Flashcard decks
- Viva answer packs
- OSCE image recognition packs
- Audio-friendly scripts
- Prompt packs

### Level 2 - Community and teaching

- Small paid classes
- Revision groups
- Case discussion sessions
- Exam coaching
- Feedback-based study circles

### Level 3 - AI-assisted tools

- Patient summary generator
- OSCE simulator
- Viva examiner chatbot
- Flashcard generator
- Ward checklist assistant

### Level 4 - Healthcare systems and devices

- Device troubleshooting guides
- Equipment workflow systems
- Inventory and error-proofing systems
- Procurement support
- Medical technology training products

## Product rule

Start with a small paid proof before building a big system.
