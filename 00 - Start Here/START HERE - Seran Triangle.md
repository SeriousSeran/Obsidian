# START HERE - Seran Triangle

This vault is a structured operating map, not a random note dump.

It is built around three connected domains:

1. **Medicine** - clinical skill, exam success, patient reasoning, credibility.
2. **Commerce and Money** - cash flow, debt control, business models, sales, investing.
3. **AI and Technology** - tools, automation, medical education products, healthcare systems.

## Core identity

> I am becoming a doctor who builds AI-assisted medical education and healthcare tools, starting with exam-focused study products and later moving into clinical workflow systems, healthcare devices, and scalable health-tech products.

## The correct order

1. Stabilize money and health.
2. Pass exams and become clinically solid.
3. Build one small useful medical education product.
4. Build a teaching and content system.
5. Learn commerce, accounting, banking, business models, sales, and investing.
6. Prototype AI-healthcare tools.
7. Later build scalable products, systems, or businesses.
