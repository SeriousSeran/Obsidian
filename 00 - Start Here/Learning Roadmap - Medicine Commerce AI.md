# Learning Roadmap - Medicine Commerce AI

This is the long-term map for Seran Triangle.

## Phase 1 - Stabilize

Before building wealth, build stability.

### Learn

- Budgeting
- Cash flow
- Debt control
- Interest
- Emergency fund
- Time protection
- Sleep and food basics

### Build

- A simple expense tracker
- A weekly money review
- A protected food and health budget
- A debt repayment list
- A study schedule that does not collapse under stress

### Avoid

- Crypto trading as a rescue plan
- Random online money schemes
- Buying courses impulsively
- Perfect planning without output

## Phase 2 - Medical base

Medicine is the trust engine.

### Learn

- Clinical reasoning
- Emergency medicine
- Case presentation
- OSCE and viva answering
- Ward-based decision making
- Common investigation interpretation
- Safe prescribing principles

### Build

- [[Clinical Exam and Viva System]]
- [[Emergency Medicine Viva Flashcard Product]]
- Deidentified clinical reasoning notes
- Audio-friendly revision scripts
- Image-based OSCE recognition notes

## Phase 3 - Commerce base

Commerce is the language of how money moves.

### Learn

- Assets and liabilities
- Income statement
- Balance sheet
- Cash-flow statement
- Gross margin
- Net margin
- Fixed cost and variable cost
- Debt and leverage
- Banking
- Sales and marketing
- Business models

### Build

- [[Personal Finance Stabilization]]
- [[Commerce MOC]]
- Product pricing notes
- Honest sales scripts

## Phase 4 - Product engine

Product income begins with a real pain and a useful solution.

### Learn

- Customer pain
- Minimum viable product
- Distribution
- Feedback loops
- Pricing
- Content funnel
- Digital product delivery
- Community building

### Build

- [[Medical Education Product Engine]]
- [[Emergency Medicine Viva Flashcard Product]]
- [[OSCE Image Recognition Product]]
- [[AI Prompt Pack for Medical Students]]

## Phase 5 - AI and healthcare tools

The rare skill is not coding alone. The rare skill is clinical product thinking.

### Learn

- Prompt engineering
- Databases
- Spreadsheets
- No-code tools
- Python basics
- APIs
- Product design
- Privacy and safety
- Healthcare workflow

### Build

- [[Healthcare AI and Devices Roadmap]]
- Patient summary generator prototype
- OSCE examiner simulator
- Emergency viva chatbot
- Ward checklist assistant
- Study deck generator

## Phase 6 - Wealth storage

First earn actively, then store intelligently, then grow patiently.

### Learn

- Emergency fund
- Fixed deposits
- Treasury bills and bonds
- Unit trusts
- Index funds where available
- Stocks
- Gold
- Real estate basics
- Diversification
- Currency risk

### Avoid

- Treating investing as gambling
- Trading because of financial stress
- Investing before stabilizing debt and food
