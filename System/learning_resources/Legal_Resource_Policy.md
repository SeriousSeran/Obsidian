# Legal Resource Policy

## Rule

This vault may link to:

- official publisher pages
- official author pages
- open textbooks
- government or university resources
- library/catalog pages
- legal purchase or borrow pages

This vault should not link to:

- pirated textbook PDFs
- Anna's Archive
- LibGen
- shadow library mirrors
- unauthorized scans

## Why

The Life OS should be durable, reviewable, and safe to maintain with agents. Legal links keep the vault useful without creating copyright or account-risk problems.
