# Health and Physique MOC

Money, intelligence, and clinical performance collapse if health collapses.

This folder is for body, energy, sleep, food, training, and sustainable discipline.

## Core principles

1. Protect sleep.
2. Protect food.
3. Protect movement.
4. Avoid using stress as identity.
5. Build a body that can survive medical training and work.

## Minimum viable health system

When life is busy, do not aim for perfect fitness.

Aim for:

- Regular meals
- Protein with meals when possible
- Hydration
- Walking
- Short strength training
- Sleep routine
- Reduced doom-scrolling
- Caffeine awareness

## Why physique matters

A better body improves:

- Energy
- Confidence
- Stress tolerance
- Study endurance
- Clinical performance
- Mood
- Long-term health
