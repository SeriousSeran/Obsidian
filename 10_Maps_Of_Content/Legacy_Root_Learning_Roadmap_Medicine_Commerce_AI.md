# Learning Roadmap - Medicine Commerce AI

This is the long-term map. It is not a one-week plan. It is a direction system.

## Phase 1 - Stabilize

Before building wealth, build stability.

Learn: budgeting, cash flow, debt control, interest, emergency fund, time protection, sleep and food basics.

Build: expense tracker, weekly money review, protected food and health budget, debt list, study schedule.

Avoid: crypto as a rescue plan, random online money schemes, impulsive course buying, perfect planning without output.

## Phase 2 - Medical base

Medicine is the trust engine.

Learn: clinical reasoning, emergency medicine, case presentation, OSCE and viva answering, ward-based decision making, investigation interpretation, safe prescribing.

Build: [[Clinical Exam and Viva System]], deidentified clinical reasoning notes, audio-friendly revision scripts, image-based OSCE recognition notes.

## Phase 3 - Commerce base

Commerce is the language of how money moves.

Learn: assets and liabilities, income statement, balance sheet, cash-flow statement, gross margin, net margin, fixed and variable costs, debt, banking, sales, marketing, business models.

Build: [[Personal Finance Stabilization]], one-page business model notes, product pricing notes, honest sales scripts.

## Phase 4 - Product engine

Product income begins with a real pain and a useful solution.

Learn: customer pain, minimum viable product, distribution, feedback loops, pricing, digital delivery, community building.

Build: [[Medical Education Product Engine]], [[Emergency Medicine Viva Flashcard Product]], [[OSCE Image Recognition Product]], [[AI Prompt Pack for Medical Students]].

## Phase 5 - AI and healthcare tools

The rare skill is not coding alone. The rare skill is clinical product thinking.

Learn: prompting, databases, spreadsheets, no-code tools, Python basics, APIs, privacy, safety, product design, healthcare workflow.

Build: patient summary generator, OSCE examiner simulator, emergency viva chatbot, ward checklist assistant, study deck generator.

## Phase 6 - Wealth storage

First earn actively, then store intelligently, then grow patiently.

Learn: emergency fund, fixed deposits, Treasury bills and bonds, unit trusts, index funds where available, stocks, gold, real estate basics, diversification, currency risk.
