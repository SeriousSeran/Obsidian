# Offer Creation Framework

> A framework to design small, testable business offers.

**Disclaimer:** This framework is for educational and planning purposes only. It does not constitute financial advice, nor does it guarantee any financial outcomes or business success.

## Core Questions

1. **Who is the target audience?**
2. **What specific problem are they facing?**
3. **What is the proposed solution (the offer)?**
4. **Why are we uniquely positioned to solve this?**
5. **What is the smallest version we can test in a week?**

## The Offer

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| Target | Who |
| Problem | What |
| Solution | How |
| Test | Smallest version |
| Success Metric | E.g. 5 pre-orders |

## Notes

- Keep the initial offer as small and testable as possible.
- Focus on validation before scaling.
