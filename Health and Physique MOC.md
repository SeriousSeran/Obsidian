# Health and Physique MOC

Money and intelligence collapse if health collapses.

This part of the vault is for body, energy, sleep, food, and training.

## Core principles

1. Protect sleep.
2. Protect food.
3. Protect movement.
4. Avoid using stress as identity.
5. Build a body that can survive medical training and work.

## Minimum viable health system

When life is busy, do not aim for perfect fitness.

Aim for regular meals, protein when possible, hydration, walking, short strength training, sleep routine, reduced doom-scrolling, and caffeine awareness.

## Why physique matters

A better body improves energy, confidence, stress tolerance, study endurance, clinical performance, mood, and long-term health.
