# Sales Without Lying

## Core idea

Sales does not have to mean manipulation.

Good sales is finding a real pain, making a real solution, explaining it clearly, and making it easy to buy.

## Why sales feels uncomfortable

Honest people often hate sales because they associate it with exaggeration, pressure, fake scarcity, and pretending. But ethical sales is closer to clinical communication.

## Honest sales script

```text
I made this because many students struggle with this exact area.

It is not a magic shortcut, but it can help you revise faster and answer better in viva or OSCE.

Here is what is inside.
Here is who it is for.
Here is who it is not for.
Here is the price.
```

## Sales principles

Name the pain clearly, show the transformation, show what is included, avoid false promises, avoid pressure, invite feedback, improve the product.
