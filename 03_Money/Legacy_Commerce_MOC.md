## The sequence

Do not start with advanced investing. Start with cash flow, debt control, expense awareness, emergency buffer, active income, semi-passive products, investing, business ownership, then equity and assets.

## The most important distinction

Income is not wealth.

Wealth comes from keeping surplus, owning assets, building systems, creating products, controlling expenses, and avoiding destructive debt.

## Medical analogy

Cash flow is circulation. Debt is bleeding. Emergency fund is reserve capacity. Investing is rehabilitation, not resuscitation.
