# Lean Experiment Framework

> This framework is for planning and experimentation only, and does not constitute financial advice.

## Concept

A methodology to test business ideas and income streams quickly with minimal risk.

## Stages

1. **Hypothesis**: What is the core assumption? Who is the customer, and what is the painful problem?
2. **MVP (Minimum Viable Product)**: What is the smallest, fastest way to test if people will pay for a solution?
3. **Execution**: Run the test for a fixed period (e.g., 1 week).
4. **Analysis**: Did the test succeed or fail? What did we learn?
5. **Decision**: Pivot, Persevere, or Abandon.
