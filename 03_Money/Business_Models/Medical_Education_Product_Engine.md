# Medical Education Product Engine

## Core idea

Medical education is the best first side-income path because it uses existing strengths: clinical study, teaching, AI-assisted organization, exam awareness, flashcard creation, explanation skill, English/Sinhala/Singlish communication, visual thinking, and systems thinking.

## First product principle

Do not start with a huge course. Start with one small product that solves one painful problem.

Examples: emergency viva flashcards, ECG recognition cards, ABG interpretation cards, OSCE image pack, case presentation templates, dengue framework, emergency drug dose cards.

## Product creation cycle

1. Pick a narrow exam pain.
2. Create a small useful version.
3. Give a sample to trusted students.
4. Ask what was useful and confusing.
5. Price modestly.
6. Improve based on feedback.
7. Convert feedback into version 2.
8. Create related content to distribute it.

## Good product structure

Each card or note should include what is seen, what it means, mechanism, immediate danger, investigations, management, viva wording, common traps, and flashcard question.

## Avoid

Waiting until perfect, making free content forever, designing logos before product, buying tools before selling, making broad notes nobody urgently needs.
