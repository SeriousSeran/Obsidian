# Assets and Liabilities

## Simple definition

An asset puts value into your life or pocket.

A liability takes value out of your life or pocket.

## Examples of assets

Skills that increase income, medical qualification, teaching products, useful software tools, emergency fund, investments, profitable business, trusted audience, systems that save time.

## Examples of liabilities

High-interest debt, unused subscriptions, impulsive purchases, tools bought without output, status spending, businesses that lose money, devices bought for identity rather than utility.

## Personal application

The first major asset is not a stock or crypto coin. The first major asset is the ability to earn reliably while keeping health, cognition, and time intact.
