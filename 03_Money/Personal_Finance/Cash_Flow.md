# Cash Flow

## Definition

Cash flow is the movement of money in and out over time. It is not the same as income.

A person can earn well and still be financially weak if money leaves faster than it enters.

## Why it matters

Cash flow is the financial equivalent of circulation. A business can look profitable but die from lack of cash.

## Formula

Money in minus money out equals surplus.

If surplus is negative, wealth building is not possible yet.

## Action

Track daily for one month using:

```text
Date - Item - Amount - Category - Necessary or avoidable
```

At the end of the week, review patterns rather than blaming yourself daily.
